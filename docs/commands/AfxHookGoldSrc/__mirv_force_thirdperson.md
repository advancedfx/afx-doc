# __mirv_force_thirdperson

<tt>__mirv_force_thirdperson **0**|1|2</tt>

Forces ''CL_IsThirdPerson''.

* **0 :** no change (default)
* **1 :** always force TRUE (1)
* **2 :** always force FALSE (0)
